High School

Our High School

At TED Kayseri College High School, the aims and responsibilities of secondary education are in accordance with the general objectives and fundamental principles of Turkish National Education:

(1) To promote and maintain social and cultural integration in line with Atatürk nationalism.

(2) To provide all students with a minimum common general culture at the secondary education level, enabling them to recognize individual and societal issues, seek solutions to these issues, and develop an awareness and capacity to contribute to the economic, social, and cultural development of society.

(3) To prepare students for higher education or vocational fields or both, considering the requirements of the society's economy, by developing their interests and abilities within various programs.
