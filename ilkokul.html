PRIMARY SCHOOL

Our Primary School

The objectives and responsibilities of primary education at TED Kayseri College Elementary School, in accordance with the general objectives and basic principles of Turkish National Education, are as follows:


(1) To teach students about Atatürk's principles and revolutions, Atatürk nationalism, and the truths underlying our social struggle.


(2) To provide children with the necessary fundamental knowledge, skills, behaviors, and habits to become good and beneficial citizens.


(3) To prepare children for social life and higher education by nurturing their interests and talents.


(4) To develop children's aesthetic sense, enable them to perceive the truth, beauty, and goodness, and foster noble feelings such as unity, cooperation, mutual assistance, friendship, and love for humanity.


Based on the principles of science, love, respect, and tolerance that form the foundation of the education system, a democratic and contemporary learning environment is provided for students. The school aims to cultivate individuals who engage in both individual and team-based learning, socialize, develop critical thinking skills, are aware of their responsibilities, abide by rules, and establish effective communication with their families, friends, teachers, and surroundings. The goal is to nurture individuals who are continuously learning, evolving, and becoming conscious of their actions.

The primary school building is equipped with classrooms designed to accommodate different learning styles, subject classrooms, a science and technology laboratory, a computer laboratory, and an information access center (library).
