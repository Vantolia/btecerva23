MIDDLE SCHOOL

Our Middle School

As teachers and administrators at TED Kayseri College, we closely monitor the social, cognitive, physiological, and academic changes and developments of our students as they transition from childhood to adolescence, interact with numerous subject teachers, and begin to stand on their own feet, taking on more responsibilities during their middle school years.
Our ultimate goal is to educate individuals who are shaping their personalities during these years, taking into account their individual differences, teaching them to respect diversity, and nurturing self-expression. We aim to cultivate creative, inquisitive, independent thinkers who integrate what they have learned with their daily life skills, and become sensitive and happy individuals.
In line with these goals, our school implements not only the national curriculum in the light of Atatürk's principles and revolutions but also unique programs accredited by the international BTEC (Business and Technology Education Council), available exclusively at TED Schools. Our strong academic staff provides project-based, student-centered teaching methods, avoiding rote memorization and focusing on scientific thinking, enabling students to develop 21st-century skills. We also create environments for students to engage in extracurricular activities, identify their social, cultural, artistic, and athletic talents, and continually improve themselves while interacting with the world.
At TED Kayseri College, during this period when communication becomes even more crucial, we ensure that students understand different cultures and can effectively use multiple languages in their lives. We provide them with opportunities to gain effective and enjoyable learning experiences through our library, sports hall, music, visual arts, technology design, innovation, information technology, culinary arts, ceramics workshops, science, and technology laboratories, as well as well-equipped classrooms. Emphasizing our institutional vision, we consider every corner of the school as a learning space, fostering lasting learning and nurturing individuals who are disciplined, global citizens.
