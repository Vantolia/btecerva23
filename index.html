Trip To Cappadocia

"Ensuring that TED Kayseri College Foundation Schools educate individuals who use at least one foreign language effectively in their lives.” Based on this mission , our annual traditional school trip called "Live it, Learn it Cappadocia!" was organized. Accompanied by licensed guides who only spoke English throughout the whole trip, our students got to know the region and were supported in both their history knowledge and language development by using English outside the curriculum. They also had a great time with the tourists in the region.

Vibrant Celebration: 19th May at TED Kayseri College

The 104th anniversary of the momentous occasion when Great Leader Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, along with his comrades-in-arms, set foot in Samsun on May 19, 1919, aboard the Bandırma Steamship to initiate our War of Independence, was celebrated at our school with a spirited ceremony.

Opening of TED Kayseri College School Museum

TED Kayseri College Museum Opens, Filled with Memories Spanning 57 Years of Our History. The museum, which was opened on March 31, 2023, with great interest and participation from the TED Kayseri College Foundation family, had its ceremonial ribbon cut by our first graduates. In order to immortalize the 57-year history of our school, which holds great significance for all of its graduates, TED Kayseri College Museum has been established. The museum proudly displays the names of all our school's alumni.

Our Sports Hall

Our semi-Olympic swimming pool has been designed using current technological capabilities and taking into account all hygiene criteria. The section serving as a Fitness Center for our students covers an area of 227 square meters. Our basketball courts have NBA-approved parquet flooring and are built to meet the standards for national competitions. These courts can also be used for volleyball and handball matches. Additionally, 8 modern dressing rooms have been constructed.

Our Conference Hall

Our thoughtfully designed venue provides a dynamic space for educational events and gatherings. Equipped with modern technology and comfortable seating, our conference hall is perfect for hosting academic conferences, workshops, and seminars. Embrace the opportunity to engage and connect with fellow educators, students, and speakers in a vibrant atmosphere that fosters collaboration and growth. Step into a space that encourages innovation and learning, and let our conference hall be the catalyst for transformative educational experiences.

Our Dining Hall

In order to ensure the healthy, balanced, and hygienic nutrition of students, menus prepared under the supervision of a food engineer are submitted for school management approval, and the monthly meal menu is shared with parents on the school website. Additionally, the monitoring of students' nutrition is carried out by the food engineer. All students are provided with a lunch consisting of four different options every day, and additionally, the first-grade primary school students are given an afternoon snack, while the preschool students receive a morning and afternoon snack. The meals and snacks are prepared in the school cafeteria by our food engineer, expert chef, and kitchen staff, taking into account the daily energy and nutritional needs to ensure they are delicious and tasty.
